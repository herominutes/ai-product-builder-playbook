# Product Review Framework

A structured system for evaluating product initiatives and ensuring high-quality product work.

Product reviews help organizations maintain product quality, strategic alignment, and execution discipline.

---

# Purpose

Product reviews help teams:

• validate product direction  
• identify risks early  
• improve product quality  
• ensure alignment with strategy  

---

# Types of Product Reviews

Product organizations typically conduct several types of reviews.

Strategy Reviews

Evaluate long-term product direction.

Questions include:

• Are we solving the right problems?  
• Are our priorities aligned with strategy?

---

Discovery Reviews

Evaluate problem understanding and opportunity assessment.

Questions include:

• Is the user problem clearly defined?  
• Have we validated the opportunity?

---

Design Reviews

Evaluate the user experience and product flows.

Questions include:

• Does this solve the user problem effectively?  
• Is the workflow intuitive?

---

Launch Reviews

Evaluate readiness before releasing features.

Questions include:

• Is the feature stable?  
• Are success metrics defined?

---

Post-Launch Reviews

Evaluate product performance after release.

Questions include:

• Did we achieve expected outcomes?  
• What did we learn?

---

# Review Evaluation Criteria

Product reviews should evaluate work across several dimensions.

User Impact

Does this solve a meaningful user problem?

Strategic Alignment

Does this support the product strategy?

Technical Feasibility

Is the system architecture sound?

Business Value

Does this drive meaningful business impact?
