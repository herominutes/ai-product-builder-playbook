# AI Product Requirements Document (AI-PRD)

A structured document used to define AI-powered product features, system behavior, and success metrics.

AI products introduce unique considerations such as model behavior, probabilistic outputs, and evaluation systems. This template extends traditional PRDs to support AI-native systems.

---

# Purpose

The AI-PRD ensures teams clearly define:

• the user problem being solved  
• the AI capability being introduced  
• system architecture requirements  
• evaluation and safety considerations  

This document aligns product, engineering, design, and AI teams before development begins.
