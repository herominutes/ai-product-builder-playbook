# Product Decision Log

A structured record of important product decisions and their rationale.

Product teams make many decisions during development. Without documentation, important context is often lost over time.

Decision logs preserve historical context and help teams understand why decisions were made.

---

# Purpose

Decision logs help teams:

• maintain transparency  
• document decision rationale  
• avoid repeating past mistakes  
• support organizational learning
