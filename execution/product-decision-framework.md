# Product Decision Framework

A structured approach for making product decisions in complex environments.

Product teams frequently face decisions with incomplete information. A consistent decision framework helps teams make clear, disciplined choices.

---

# Purpose

Product decision frameworks help teams:

• evaluate product opportunities  
• balance trade-offs  
• align stakeholders around decisions  

Structured decision processes improve product outcomes.

---

# Decision Principles

Effective product decisions consider:

User Impact  
Strategic Alignment  
Business Value  
Technical Feasibility  
Risk

---

# Decision Process

Problem Definition  
↓  
Opportunity Assessment  
↓  
Solution Exploration  
↓  
Impact Evaluation  
↓  
Decision

---

# Decision Types

Product teams typically make several types of decisions.

Strategic Decisions

Long-term product direction.

Product Decisions

Feature prioritization and roadmap choices.

Execution Decisions

How teams implement product initiatives.

---

# Decision Documentation

Important decisions should be documented.

Decision logs typically include:

• decision summary  
• context and rationale  
• alternatives considered  
• final outcome

Documentation improves transparency and organizational learning.
