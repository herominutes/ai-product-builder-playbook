# Product Roadmap Framework

A framework for planning and communicating product development priorities.

Product roadmaps provide visibility into product direction and upcoming initiatives.

Roadmaps help align teams around product priorities.

---

# Purpose

The goal of product roadmaps is to:

• communicate product direction  
• align teams around priorities  
• guide product development planning

---

# Roadmap Structure

Most product roadmaps organize work into themes or initiatives.

Example structure:

Now  
Next  
Later

Or

Quarter 1  
Quarter 2  
Quarter 3

---

# Roadmap Principles

Effective roadmaps:

• communicate outcomes rather than features  
• remain flexible as priorities evolve  
• align with product strategy

---

# Roadmap Development Process

Opportunity Identification  
↓  
Prioritization  
↓  
Roadmap Planning  
↓  
Execution

---

# Roadmap Communication

Product roadmaps should be shared with:

• engineering teams  
• leadership  
• cross-functional partners

Clear communication ensures alignment across the organization.
